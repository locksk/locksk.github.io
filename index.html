about.

Siobhan is a photographer based in South Wales who specialises in Live Music, Landscape and Lifestyle photography.

She is also studying at the Centre for Neuropsychiatric Genetics and Genomics, Cardiff Univeristy, where she is researching predictors of treatment outcomes in schizophrenia. She is funded by a John Grace QC PhD Scholarship 2022 award by Mental Health Research UK, and we thank them for supporting this research.
